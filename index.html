Our History

The Institute Innovation Council

Established in 2020, the IIC is the culmination of NITT's rich entrepreneurial legacy—from the E-Cell (2005) to CEDI (2012)—working seamlessly to foster innovation and realize the vision of Atmanirbhar Bharat.

About the Programme

Students identify real campus problems. IIC helps build real solutions.

The NITT Innovation Challenge by IIC, NIT Trichy asks you to look at campus life, find what's broken, and propose how to fix it. This edition's focus: the alumni disconnect.
